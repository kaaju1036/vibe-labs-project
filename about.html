Our History

From humble beginnings in a small garage, SmartTech has grown into a global technology leader. Our journey has been marked by innovation, resilience, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

2010: SmartTech is founded with a vision to revolutionize mobile communication.

2012: Launched our first smartphone, the "Innovator," which quickly gained popularity for its innovative features.

2015: Expanded our product line to include tablets and smartwatches.

2018: Opened our first international office in London.

2020: Introduced our flagship smartphone, the "Vision," featuring cutting-edge camera technology and AI capabilities.

2023: Celebrated our 13th anniversary and reaffirmed our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction.

Present: Continuing to develop cutting-edge mobile solutions to enhance the lives of people around the globe.
